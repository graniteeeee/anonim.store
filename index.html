Everyday trading, quietly on-chain.

Most web3 marketplaces are built for people who already know what a wallet, a gas fee, or a smart contract is. anonim.store isn't. It's a classifieds board for everyday buying, selling, and posting — where the Polygon network runs underneath, handling ownership and trust, while you just see a simple listing.

Every listing lives as its own NFT, which means it can carry more than a title and a photo: a location, a fixed asking price, and an encrypted file attached directly to the post — all provable, all yours, without a single technical step on your side.
