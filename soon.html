privacy

The platform doesn't know who you are.

A wallet opens with no personal data. The blockchain records wallet addresses — not names, not phone numbers, not locations. What information ends up in a listing is each person's own business.

Aja added a city and category — to be found. Gugi didn't. The system works the same for both.

The first creator's wallet address stays on the blockchain. Every subsequent buyer's address is visible on the blockchain too — but the platform doesn't link it to a person. How anonymous you remain depends on what you put in the listing and how you acquired POL.

freedom and responsibility

This is not a warning. This is part of the system.

The blockchain gives a freedom that didn't exist before — to publish, trade, receive payments without a middleman who can shut you down, block you or ask who you are. This freedom is real. And it works the same in Vilnius, Bradford, or Zimbabwe.

But the blockchain is a public ledger. Transactions are visible — just an address, not a person. Anonymity means the platform doesn't link your address to your identity — not that transactions are invisible. It's a tool, not a shield.

The creator is responsible for published content under the laws of their country. The platform doesn't control this — and can't. That's also part of the freedom.

faq

Questions, answered.

What is POL and how do I get it?

POL is the Polygon network currency. Buy it at global.transak.com — accepts regular bank cards. Transak will ask you to verify your identity — standard procedure, takes about 5 minutes. We recommend starting with €4. That's enough for up to 2,500 listings.

Do I really not need to register?

Correct. PIN code and 12-word key — that's it. No email, no phone. The same key restores your wallet on any device — in anonim.store, MetaMask or Trust Wallet.

How much does it cost to create a listing?

Two separate costs: the Polygon network transaction (~0.0015 of a cent) and the platform tool fee — 5% of your listed price, once at the moment of creation. Minimum listing price — 0.001 POL. No fee on resales.

How long does a listing last?

Forever. Only the owner can destroy it using the burn function — transaction cost around 0.001 POL.

Can I resell a listing I bought?

Yes. A listing is a digital asset. The person who bought your coupon or instructions can later resell that access to someone else — like passing on a gift card.

How does a buyer find my listing?

Through filters — city, topic, maximum price. Filters are the main discovery mechanism.

How do I install the APK?

Download from anonim.store. Android will ask you to allow installation from unknown sources — go to Settings → Apps → Allow from this source. This is normal — we are not on Google Play.

What file types can I upload?

Images (PNG, JPG, GIF), audio (MP3, FLAC, WAV), video (MP4, WEBM), 3D models (GLTF, GLB). A secret unlock part can be added to any format.

What if I lose my phone?

Install the app on any Android device and enter your 12-word key. If you lose the key — access cannot be recovered.
